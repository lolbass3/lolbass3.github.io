About Me

Lowell Ringel is a bassist, educator, and composer originally from Hillsborough, North Carolina. Performing regularly across Florida and North Carolina, he is in his final year at the Frost School of Music in Miami for instrumental jazz performance. In his young career, he has performed with both the Duke University and University of Miami Big Bands and is currently a member of the award-winning vocal group "Extensions" directed by Dr. Kate Reid. He additionally has shared the stage with artists such as Slide Hampton, Stephen Riley, Jim Gasior, Gretchen Parlato, and Carmen Lundy at venues throughout the East Coast. He truly is just beginning to make his impact on the national jazz landscape, winning three 2015 Downbeat Magazine Student awards and earning first place at the 2015 Monterrey Next Generation Jazz Festival.
